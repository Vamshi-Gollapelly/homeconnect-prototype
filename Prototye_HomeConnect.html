HomeConnect

Structured prototype based on the full concept document.

✨ Complete settlement support ecosystem

Not just an app. A guided platform for the whole newcomer journey.

HomeConnect is designed to help international students, visa holders, and first-time arrivals handle the most difficult first days in a new country. It brings together transport, temporary stay, groceries, SIM setup, bank guidance, local orientation, trust checks, real-time support, and protected payments in one organised product flow.

Airport pickup Temporary stay Grocery starter kits SIM setup Bank & Medicare help City orientation

Problem

New arrivals face many urgent tasks at once and currently rely on disconnected apps or unreliable informal help.

Response

HomeConnect connects services, guidance, trust, and real-time support in one platform.

Stakeholder Value

It blends Uber-style coordination, Airbnb-style trust, and wallet-based transaction control into a practical ecosystem.

Core idea and platform logic

This section clearly shows what HomeConnect really is, so the prototype does not feel like random features.

Core product meaning
One journey, not many disconnected tools
A user should not move between transport apps, housing apps, payment tools, and informal contacts. HomeConnect keeps the whole first-mile settlement process inside one structured experience.
Support from arrival to stability
The app is designed to support what the user needs first, next, and later. It follows the real order of settlement needs instead of showing isolated service menus.
Trust-led operations
The platform is built around verification, ratings, live tracking, support escalation, and protected payments so the service feels dependable in real life.

Feature-level clarity

Trust and safety model

These are the controls that make the product feel real, reliable, and acceptable to users and stakeholders.

Acceptance drivers

For newcomers

  • Verified helper identities and visible badges.
  • Ratings, reviews, and task history.
  • Live tracking, chat support, and SOS access during active tasks.
  • Clear prices and protected payment completion.

For platform control

  • Admin visibility across bookings and disputes.
  • Document verification and operational moderation.
  • Refund handling, quality management, and safety escalation.
  • Structured transaction release through the wallet flow.
